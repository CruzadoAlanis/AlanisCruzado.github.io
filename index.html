About Me

Hey there, I’m Alanis Cruzado! My friends and family would tell you I’m all about kindness, enthusiasm, and a serious drive to make things happen.

I thrive on working closely with groups of people, connecting with them on a personal level. Yet, I also relish the challenge of independently figuring things out. There's a unique satisfaction in tackling problems on my own—it feels like unraveling a complex puzzle. When I finally crack it, the sense of accomplishment is incredibly gratifying.

Life's a journey, and I'm constantly seeking opportunities to enhance my quality of life while pursuing challenges that advance my personal and professional growth.

Although I could chat about my passions endlessly, I'm eager for you to dive into my data visualization projects. Feel free to check them out—I'm excited to share the insights they reveal!
